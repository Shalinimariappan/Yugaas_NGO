Maha Akshaya Sivam Trust
“Empowering Lives,
Embracing Souls”

Maha Akshaya Sivam Trust had its humble beginnings in the
hearts of devoted individuals who yearned to make a
difference in the lives of the less fortunate. Inspired by the
teachings and ideals of revered spiritual leaders—Kanchi
Maha Periva, Yogi Ramsuratkumar, Gnanananda Swamigal,
and Sri Sivan Saar—our trust was founded with a deep-
rooted commitment to serving humanity.

The Vision Takes Shape

The seeds of compassion were sown when like-minded
devotees came together, united by the shared dream of
creating an equitable society. Fuelled by the vision of a
world where everyone could access basic needs such as
Food, Education, and Medical Facilities, Maha Akshaya
Sivam Trust took its first steps on the path of service.

Our Founders and
Managing Truste

Our journey was nurtured and guided by our dedicated
founders and the visionary Managing Trustee. Their
unwavering dedication and selfless spirit laid the foundation
for the trust's transformative impact. With their blessings,
Maha Akshaya Sivam Trust embarked on a noble endeavor
to empower lives and embrace souls.

Growing Compassion,
Growing Impact

Over the years, the trust's efforts expanded, and its
impact grew exponentially. With the support of kind-
hearted individuals, organizations, and governmental
agencies, we were able to reach more communities in
need. Our services evolved to encompass diverse
initiatives, all centered around the core themes of Food,
Education, and Medical Facilities.

Flagship Service:
Honoring Departed Souls

We are dedicated to the sacred duty of performing dignified
last rites for unclaimed bodies. This flagship service reflects
our belief in the inherent dignity of every human being,
ensuring that no one departs this world unremembered. Our
commitment to compassion and inclusivity drives us in our
mission to create a world where basic needs and respect
are extended to all.

A Beacon of Hope

The Journey Continues

As we continue on our transformative journey, the trust
remains committed to its mission of serving humanity with
love and selflessness. We are eternally grateful to all our
supporters, volunteers, donors, and partners who have
contributed to the growth and impact of Maha Akshaya
Sivam Trust.
