Volunteer Opportunities and

How to Join

At Maha Akshaya Sivam Trust, we believe that each volunteer is a torchbearer of change, illuminating lives with their kindness and dedication. If you wish to be a part of our initiatives and contribute your time and talents towards serving humanity, we welcome you with open arms.

Step 1: Express Your Interest

To embark on this transformative journey, express your interest in volunteering by reaching out to us through our website or contacting our volunteer coordinator directly.

Step 2: Explore Opportunities

Our volunteer coordinator will guide you through the diverse range of volunteer opportunities available. Discover roles that align with your skills, interests, and availability.

Step 3: Volunteer Orientation

Once you've selected your preferred role, participate in our comprehensive volunteer orientation program. Learn about the trust's mission, values, and initiatives.

Step 4: Impact and Gratitude

Witness the impact of your efforts as you touch lives and inspire change. Experience the joy of knowing that your selfless service is illuminating souls with love and hope.

Together, Let's Illuminate Souls

At Maha Akshaya Sivam Trust, our volunteers are the heartbeat of our initiatives. By joining us, you become a part of a compassionate family that aims to make the world a better place for all. Together, let's illuminate souls with love, compassion, and the promise of a brighter future.
