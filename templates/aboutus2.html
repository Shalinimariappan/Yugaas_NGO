Our Motto Harmony in Service :

Embracing "Shreyo Bhooyath, Sakala jananaam"

The motto "Shreyo Bhooyath, Sakala jananaam" is a profound and inspiring Sanskrit phrase that
embodies the guiding principle and aspiration of the trust. This motto is derived from the
renowned song "Maitreem Bhajatha," which was penned by the revered sage, Kanchi Maha
Periva. The song conveys a beautiful message of universal brotherhood and emphasizes the
importance of promoting peace, love, and harmony among all people.

In the context of the trust, Embracing This Motto signifies the trust's commitment to serving
humanity with love and compassion, without any discrimination based on caste, creed, or
community. It reflects the inclusive and egalitarian approach of the trust, where the welfare
and well-being of all individuals are valued and prioritized.

The timeless wisdom of "Maitreem Bhajatha" continues to inspire countless people to work
towards a more harmonious and compassionate world. By incorporating this motto into its
mission, the trust seeks to infuse the spirit of the song into its service and outreach, truly
reflecting the teachings of the great sage, Kanchi Maha Periva.

Through the embodiment of "Shreyo Bhooyath, Sakala jananaam," the trust aims to inspire and
instill a sense of responsibility and empathy in individuals, encouraging them to contribute to
the collective well-being and upliftment of society as a whole. The trust's dedication to living
by this motto exemplifies its vision of creating a more equitable and compassionate society
where every individual is treated with love, respect, and kindness.

As we continue to uphold the values of "Maitreem Bhajatha" in our endeavors, we strive to
spread the message of universal love and harmony, illuminating souls with hope and
compassion for a brighter future.
